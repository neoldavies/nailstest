100% HEMA-Free Chemistry

Cheap acrylics rely on HEMA (Hydroxyethyl Methacrylate), a known allergen that causes contact dermatitis and nail bed separation. We exclusively use medical-grade, HEMA-free gels to protect your long-term health.

Anhydrous Preparation

Water expands the nail plate. Traditional wet manicures guarantee your polish will chip as the nail shrinks back. Our dry, anhydrous prep ensures a flawless, mechanical bond that locks out lifting and peeling.
